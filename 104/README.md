# Mechanics of Materials Open Educational Resources

Below are links to free, openly licensed materials for studying mechanics of materials.

- [Mechanics of Materials](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/materials-science-and-engineering/3-11-mechanics-of-materials-fall-1999/) by David Roylance (MIT OpenCourseWare, CC BY-NC-SA)
- [Solid Mechanics](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/civil-and-environmental-engineering/1-050-solid-mechanics-fall-2004/) MIT OpenCourseWare (CC BY-NC-SA)
- [Mechanics of Materials](https://eng.libretexts.org/Bookshelves/Mechanical_Engineering/Mechanics_of_Materials_%28Roylance%29) (LibreTexts, CC BY-NC-SA)
- [Engineering Mechanics: Statics](https://open.umn.edu/opentextbooks/textbooks/1283) (Open Textbook, CC BY-NC-SA)
- [College Physics 2e](https://openstax.org/books/college-physics-2e/pages/4-introduction-to-dynamics-newtons-laws-of-motion) (OpenStax, CC BY 4.0)
